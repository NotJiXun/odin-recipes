Spaghetti Chicken Casserole

The Only Spaghetti Chicken Casserole Recipe You'll Ever Need

This cheesey, one-pan meal is the perfect weeknight comfort food. It combines tender spaghetti and chicken in a creamy sauce with a hint of white wine and a double layer of melted Cheddar. Just mix everything together, bake until bubbly, and top with extra cheese. It's a simple, hearty dish that's always a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup chopped pimento peppers
  • ¼ cup chopped green bell pepper
  • ¼ cup white wine
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps/Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 10 to 12 minutes.
  3. Drain spaghetti and transfer to a large bowl. Add chicken, 1 1/2 cups Cheddar cheese, condensed soup, onion, pimentos, bell pepper, wine, salt, and pepper. Mix until well combined, then spread mixture into a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 minutes. Sprinkle remaining 1/2 cup Cheddar over casserole and bake until melted, about 15 minutes longer.
