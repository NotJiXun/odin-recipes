Hungarian Mushroom Soup

The Only Mushroom Soup Recipe You'll Ever Need

This creamy, savory soup is packed with fresh mushrooms and flavored with paprika and dill. It's a simple, one-pot meal that's rich, earthy, and perfect for a chilly day. Just sauté the mushrooms, simmer with broth, and stir in sour cream at the end for a velvety finish. It's a cozy classic that tastes like it took hours to make, but comes together in no time .

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cups chopped onions
  • 1 pound fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 2 teaspoons dried dill weed
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • ¼ cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ground black pepper to taste
